## Recommended — Planning Plate

Create a premium iOS app icon for Foodiary, a local-first calorie tracker and daily meal planner. Foodiary helps users create an estimated daily calorie target and plan meals across breakfast, lunch, snack, and dinner. The visual concept is an abstract plate that also reads as a four-segment daily planning ring, with one small sky-blue target marker representing the estimated planning target. The brand should feel calm, refined, data-forward, and supportive — not medical, not clinical, not diet-shaming, not childish. Use a deep ink-to-blue gradient field with Foodiary’s Pulse v2 colors: sky blue #4BB8FA, deep ink #15142A, soft off-white #F6F7FF, subtle cyan and mint accents. Centered geometric glyph, crisp at small sizes, subtle Apple-style depth, clean rounded-square app icon composition. No text, no letters, no numbers, no utensils clutter, no medical symbols, no realistic food photography.
